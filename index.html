Features
Everything you need
A simple, powerful tool built for ministry work — no clutter, just what matters.
📅

Hour Tracking

Log your ministry hours by day. See monthly totals at a glance and send your report with one tap.

📖

Ministry Planning

Plan your ministry schedule in advance. Color-coded days show you at a glance if you hit your targets.

📚

Bible Studies

Track the number of Bible studies per month — simple counter, always at your fingertips.

📊

Charts & Progress

Visualize your ministry year with beautiful bar charts. Track your full service year (September–August).

🔔

Smart Reminders

Morning reminder with today's plan at 9 AM. Evening reminder to log your hours at 8 PM.

🔲

Home Screen Widget

See today's planned hours and this month's total right on your home screen — no need to open the app.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Your data syncs automatically across all your Apple devices. Switch between iPhone and iPad seamlessly — everything stays in sync.

🔒

Face ID & Touch ID

Keep your ministry data private. Enable biometric lock and the app requires authentication every time you open it.

💾

Backup & Restore

Export your data as a backup file at any time. Restore it on any device — your data is always safe.

🌙

Dark Mode Only

Designed exclusively for dark mode — easy on the eyes, day or night. A clean, focused experience with a beautiful purple accent.
